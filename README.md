# Food Restaurant App 🍽️🛒

The Food Delivery Application is a user-friendly mobile app that allows customers to order food and have it conveniently delivered to their doorstep. With just a few taps on their phone, users can explore
variety of dishes, place orders, and track their delivery in real-time. The app simplifies the food ordering process, enhances customer satisfaction.

# Features 😎👌
+Browse Categories
+View Food Items
+Add to Cart
+View Food Details
+Browse Food Items
